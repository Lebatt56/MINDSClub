Our History

The 𝙼𝙸𝙽𝙳𝚂 𝙲𝚕𝚞𝚋 was founded on December 8, 2023, by the students of the first cohort of Data Science at the Professional University Institute of Nouakchott. The name "MINDS" is an acronym for Machine Intelligence, Networks, and Data Science, reflecting the club's focus on these core areas.

Vision

To create an active community passionate about data science and information technology, promoting knowledge exchange and fostering continuous development.

Mission

  • Enhance members' skills in data science, artificial intelligence, and IT through workshops and collaborative projects.
  • Promote innovation and critical thinking among the university community.
  • Encourage networking and collaboration between students and industry professionals.
